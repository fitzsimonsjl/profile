Saas247 - Group

Ruby on Rails | Figma | Heroku | Bootstrap | PostgreSQL | JavaScript | HTML & CSS
Saas247 was our group’s final project as part of the Le Wagon 9 week web development boot camp. Each group is required to pitch and build an MVP. Saas247 is a way for small to medium sized companies to keep track of their Saas license costs. They can see how their license costs have increased over time, license usage across departments, and where potential savings can be made based upon the number of licenses purchased and the number of licenses in use. My role in Saas247 was on front end, designing cards to display metrics, as well as implementing graph views on the dashboard for costs over time and license utilisation by department. Graphs were implemented using Chart.js and Chartkick.
